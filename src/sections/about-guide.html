About the Guide

Purpose

This comprehensive guide provides essential information for living, governing, and enjoying life in Blue Mountain. Whether you're a new resident learning about our community or a longtime homeowner seeking specific contact information, this booklet serves as your primary reference for all aspects of mountain life.

How to Use This Guide

Refer to the Table of Contents to locate key sections quickly. Each section is designed to be both comprehensive and easy to navigate, with cross-references to related information throughout the guide.

Major Sections Include:

  • Governance: Board structure, committees, and administrative details
  • Safety: Fire safety, emergency preparedness, and construction requirements
  • Services: Community amenities, waste disposal, and utility information
  • Recreation: The Lodge, Deer Lake, hiking trails, and social activities
  • Natural Environment: Wildlife safety, native plants, and conservation
  • Local Attractions: Wineries, scenic areas, and area highlights

Quick Reference

For immediate needs:

  • Emergencies: Call 911, then see Emergency Contacts section
  • Board Meetings: Second Monday of each month, 6:00 PM at The Lodge
  • Website: BMPOA.org for current announcements and forms
  • General Questions: Contact any board member or committee chair

Keeping Information Current

This guide is updated annually. For the most current information on events, board decisions, and community announcements, please visit BMPOA.org or join our Facebook groups listed in the Communication section.
