A Mountain Home

Created in the late 1950's as a vacation community for Washington D.C. residents, Blue Mountain is now a mix of summer residents and year-round families. Renowned for scenic overlooks, seasonal wildflowers, and a peaceful mountain retreat, the subdivision is a Sanitary District in Warren County, managed by an elected volunteer executive board.
